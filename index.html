Welcome to Luigi's!

Where every bite is filled with tradition.

At Luigi's, we bring the heart of Italy to your table. From handmade pasta to wood-fired pizzas, every dish is crafted with passion, tradition, and the freshest ingredients.

Luigi's Favorites

Wood-Fired Pizza

  • Margherita Classica: San Marzano tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and basil on a crispy Neapolitan crust.
  • Prosciutto & Arugula: Thinly sliced prosciutto, arugula, and shaved parmesan on a light tomato base.

Pasta

  • Tagliatelle al Tartufo: Handmade ribbon pasta tossed in a rich truffle cream sauce, topped with shaved parmesan.
  • Linguine Frutti di Mare: A seafood lover’s dream with shrimp, mussels, and calamari in a white wine garlic sauce.

Desserts

  • Affogato al Caffè: A scoop of creamy vanilla gelato "drowned" in a shot of hot espresso, the perfect mix of hot and cold.
  • Ricotta Cheesecake: A classic Italian-style cheesecake made with ricotta and a hint of citrus, served with a berry compote.
